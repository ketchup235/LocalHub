How to use LocalHub

Interactive Q&A Guide

Q: How do I find businesses?

A: Enter your 5-digit US Zip Code in the search bar. Our system will securely hydrate our database with fresh, verified local businesses from your area.

Q: What are the floating shapes?

A: They are interactive category filters! Click them to automatically filter your local results by Food, Retail, or Services.

Q: How do I save or export my favorites?

A: Click the heart icon to save a business to your local device. You can then click the "Download Saved Businesses (PDF)" button to export a formatted report of your favorites!
